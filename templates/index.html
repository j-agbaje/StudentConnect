About StudentConnect

StudentConnect is a web platform to support international students targeting students going to universities where the language barrier between English the main language of instruction and the common language of communication is particularly potent. The platform will provide features such as personalized profiles and language-based community groups. It aims to connect students and foster a more welcoming academic environment by reducing social isolation and strengthening peer support networks.
